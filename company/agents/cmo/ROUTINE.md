# CMO Routine

## Weekly Cadence

### Monday

- Review community signals and product positioning.
- Check whether public messaging still matches the project reality.
- Align with CEO on priorities.

### Wednesday

- Support hiring communication and external outreach.
- Review launch or announcement drafts.
- Check whether docs and messaging tell the same story.

### Friday

- Summarize community growth and messaging risks.
- Review what should be announced publicly.
- Prepare any external update needed for the next week.

---

## Success Signals

- The project has a clear public story.
- Hiring communication stays consistent.
- Community-facing messaging stays honest and current.
