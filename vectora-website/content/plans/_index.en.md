Choose the plan that best fits your team size and data sovereignty needs. We offer everything from the **Free** plan for individual developers to on-premise **Enterprise** solutions.

## Plan Comparison

| Feature                       | Free             | Plus                   | Team                   |
| :---------------------------- | :--------------- | :--------------------- | :--------------------- |
| **Price**                     | Free             | $29/month              | Custom                 |
| **Vectora Cognitive Runtime** | Local (Included) | Managed Plus           | Managed Plus           |
| **Models**                    | BYOK             | BYOK or Plus (Managed) | BYOK or Plus (Managed) |
| **Users**                     | 1                | Up to 50               | Unlimited              |
| **SLA**                       | -                | 99.9%                  | 99.99%                 |
| **Deployment**                | Local/Cloud      | Managed Cloud          | Cloud / On-Premise     |

## Plan Details

- [**Free**](./free.md) — For personal use and small projects.
- [**Plus**](./plus.md) — For startups and growing teams.
- [**Team**](./team.md) — For organizations with compliance and security requirements.
